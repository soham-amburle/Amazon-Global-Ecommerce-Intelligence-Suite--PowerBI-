# Marketing & Acquisition Performance Dashboard

**Organization:** Amazon (Simulated Enterprise E-Commerce Model)  
**Tool:** Power BI  
**Dataset:** Synthetic Global E-Commerce Dataset – 100,000 Transaction Records  
**Dashboard Focus:** Marketing Performance, Channel Contribution, Geographic Revenue Distribution, and Acquisition Trends  
**Date:** 17–22 February 2026  
**Created by:** Soham S. Amburle  

---

## Overview

The **Marketing & Acquisition Performance Dashboard** provides a strategic analytical view of marketing effectiveness within a global e-commerce business model inspired by Amazon. Built using a synthetic dataset of 100,000 transaction-level records, this dashboard focuses on evaluating revenue contribution, acquisition trends, channel performance, and geographic impact.

The primary objective of this dashboard is to help executive leadership and marketing stakeholders answer key strategic questions:

- Which marketing channels drive the most revenue?
- What percentage of revenue comes from paid vs organic sources?
- How is order acquisition trending over time?
- Which countries generate the highest marketing-driven revenue?
- How do customer segments and sales channels impact marketing performance?

By combining executive KPI monitoring, interactive filtering, geographic mapping, revenue distribution visuals, and trend analysis, the dashboard delivers a comprehensive and executive-ready marketing performance view.

---

## Business Storyline & Analytical Flow

### 1. Executive Marketing Snapshot – KPI Cards

The dashboard opens with eight KPI cards providing a high-level summary of acquisition and revenue performance:

- **Total Revenue**
- **Total Orders**
- **Total Quantity Sold**
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**
- **Gross Profit**
- **Gross Margin (%)**
- **Paid Revenue (%)**
- **Organic Revenue**

These KPIs establish immediate visibility into revenue scale, acquisition volume, profitability, and marketing channel contribution.

---

### 2. Interactive Filtering Controls – Slicers

All visuals dynamically respond to the following slicers:

- **Date (Between Range Filter)**
- **Marketing Channel**
- **Sales Channel (Web / Mobile App)**
- **Customer Segment (Prime / Non-Prime)**
- **Customer Country**

These controls allow dynamic campaign analysis across time, geography, channel mix, and customer type.

---

### 3. Channel Performance Overview – Revenue by Marketing Channel

**Revenue by Marketing Channel – Primary Performance Visual**

- Axis: Marketing Channel  
- Values: Total Revenue  

This visual highlights:

- Top-performing marketing channels  
- Revenue concentration across channels  
- Relative strength of paid vs organic acquisition  
- Channel-level performance comparison  

It forms the foundation of marketing effectiveness analysis.

---

### 4. Acquisition Momentum – Order Trend Analysis

**Order Acquisition Trend – Line Chart**

- X-Axis: Date  
- Y-Axis: Total Orders  

This visual provides insight into:

- Order growth trajectory  
- Seasonal fluctuations  
- Campaign-driven spikes  
- Consistency of acquisition performance  

It enables leadership to assess whether marketing strategies are driving sustained growth.

---

### 5. Geographic Revenue Impact – Map Visualization

**Revenue by Country – Map**

- Location: Customer Country  
- Size: Total Revenue  
- Tooltips: Total Orders, AOV, Paid Revenue (%)  

This visual identifies:

- High-performing geographic markets  
- Regional marketing impact  
- Revenue distribution across countries  
- Strategic expansion opportunities  

It introduces a global perspective to acquisition performance.

---

### 6. Channel Mix Distribution – Donut Chart

**Revenue Distribution by Marketing Channel – Donut Chart**

- Legend: Marketing Channel  
- Values: Total Revenue  
- Data Labels: Percentage of Total  

This visual communicates:

- Channel contribution share  
- Paid vs organic mix balance  
- Marketing dependency concentration  
- Strategic diversification insights  

It provides an intuitive and executive-friendly summary of channel mix.
